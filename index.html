About Me

For the past five years I have worked as a web developer with experience researching, designing and building forms and interfaces in the research and evaluation industry. I have worked on a range of projects on behalf of federal and provincial clients including Citizenship and Immigration Canada, BC Stats and the GVRD. With my background in geographic information science and data visualization, I have helped developed a travel survey product that has been successfully deployed in several major metropolitan areas in Canada. Additionally, I have participated in the design and administration of projects in areas as diverse as labour market and health systems evaluations.
